SECTION 2 – TYPES OF INFORMATION WRITERS’ GARAGE COLLECT

Writers’ Garage collect the following types of information about you

2.1. Information You Provide Writers’ Garage

We may ask for customized information such as username, your first and last name,phone number, physical address, billing address, and e-mail address when you register for our services on www.writersgarage.net. or if you contact us by filling the contact form on our website or if you subscribe to our newsletter.We use this information to operate, maintain, and provide you with the functionality of our services. We may also use this information to correspond with you and to address any issues you raise about your purchases.

2.2. Information Writers’ Garage collect from you automatically

We may directly collect analytics data or use third-party analytics tools such as google analytics to help us measure traffic and usage trends for our services. These tools collect information sent by your browser or mobile device, including the pages you visit and other information that assists us in improving our services. We collect and use this analytics information in a collective form such that it cannot reasonably be manipulated to identify any specific individual user.

2.3. Cookies information

When you visit www.writersgarage.net.we may send one or more cookies. Kindly read our cookies policy for more information.

2.4. Log File Information

Logfile information is automatically reported by your browser or mobile device each time you access our website. When you use our website, our servers automatically record certain log file information. These server logs may include anonymous information such as your web request, Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, browser type, referring / exit pages and URLs, the number of clicks and how you interact with links on the Service, domain names, landing pages, pages viewed, and other such information.

2.5. Device Identifiers

A device identifier may convey information to us about how you browse and use the Service. A device identifier may remain persistently on your device, to help you log in faster and enhance your navigation through the website. Some features of the website may not function properly if the use or availability of device identifiers is impaired or disabled. Thus, when you access the Service by or through a mobile device (including but not limited to smart-phones or tablets), we may access, collect, monitor, and/or remotely store one or more “device identifiers,” such as a universally unique identifier (“UUID”). Device identifiers are small data files or similar data structures stored on or associated with your mobile device, which uniquely identify your mobile device. A device identifier may be data stored in connection with the device hardware, data stored in connection with the device’s operating system or other software, or data sent to the device by Writers’ Garage.

2.6. Location Data

Location data may convey to us information about how you browse and use the website. When you access the Service by or through a mobile device, we would ask you for your location data and access, collect, monitor, and/or remotely store “location data,” which may include GPS coordinates (e.g. latitude and/or longitude) or similar information regarding the location of your mobile device. Some features of the website, especially location-based services, may not function properly if the use or availability of location data is impaired or disabled.

SECTION 3 – COOKIES POLICY

Our website collects information through cookies. A cookie can be defined as a string of certain information that is stored on a visitor’s or user’s computer and such information is provided to a website every time the user or visitor returns to such website. Our website uses cookies in order to identify and help to track the users of our website. There are many types of cookies, however, most commonly only two types of cookies are used. Session cookies are temporary cookies that are deleted once the user exits the browser while the permanent cookies remain in the users’ or visitor’s browser until they expire or until they are deleted by the users or visitors.

3.1. Usage Of Cookies

Our website uses cookies in a range of ways to improve your experience on our website, including keeping you signed in, or understanding how you use our website. Users have an option for cookies to not be collected by the browsers and this can be done by going into the specific browser’s settings and making sure the collection of the cookies is disabled.

We may use different types of cookies such as functionality cookie which helps the websites to possible to recognize the users/visitors on the websites and remember any previously selected preferences. These could include what language you prefer and the location you are in. We may also use advertising cookies to collect information about your visit to our website, the content you viewed, the links you followed, and information about your browser, device, and your IP address. We may share some limited aspects of this data with third parties for advertising purposes. We may also share online data collected through cookies with our advertising partners. This means that when you visit another website, you may be shown advertising based on your browsing patterns on our website.

3.2. Managing And Removing Cookies

You can set your browser not to accept cookies, and allaboutcookies.org tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, in a few cases, some of our website features may not function as a result.

Definition 

In this Agreement the words:

“Customer” “subscriber” means “a person who subscribes or purchases Writers’ Garage services.”

 

Payment

Writers’ Garage provides flexibility in the provision of its services and it is for this exact reason that payments can be made at once or split in two parts. Where a payment is made at once, it must be done immediately before inception of the provision of the services. Where a payment is split in two parts, the first instalment must be paid immediately before the inception of the provision of the services and the second instalment must be paid at any time during the provision of the services but not exceeding 8 weeks from the date the first instalment was paid.  However, where the services have been completed before 8 weeks from the date of the first instalment, the second instalment must be paid immediately before the delivery of the file(s) to the customer for proofreading. 

 

Text Submission 

Due to the nature of our services all files submitted to us must be the FINAL manuscript of the customer or subscriber. Once the file containing the text of the customer or subscriber’s manuscript has been submitted to us, there shall be NO AMENDMENTS OR ALTERATIONS to the text under any and all circumstances. There shall be NO EXCEPTIONS TO THIS PROVISION.  Once we commence work on the internal layout of the manuscript, the customer or subscriber cannot make revisions. Nevertheless, if the customer insists on making changes or alterations to the manuscript after submission, such alterations shall attract an additional fee of 80 euro. The additional fee shall be paid immediately before the changed or altered manuscript will be accepted.

With the only obvious exception of proof corrections (the corrections to layout, design and residual text inaccuracies, submitted by the customer after the final proofreading) no corrections or amendments of any type to the text can be submitted by the customer. 

Book Cover

Our service under *book cover* is limited to the creation of the cover offered in the packages on our website which entails 2 hours work of:

  • Editing;
  • Adjusting;
  • Placing the title over the pictures;
  • Colour correction;
  • Editing for the print and the ebook;
  • Adjusting the back cover with blurb and bar code etc. 

This service is provided on an image at high resolution provided by the customer or selected by the customer or selected by us following the criteria suggested by the customer in our database of images for free commercial use. 

Cover Graphic Design Work 

Any other work which is not specified in *book cover* will be charged separately. The specific quantity of work will be measured by our graphic designer and communicated to the customer once the customer explains the requirements. The appropriate service to be purchased will be “Graphic Design work” on our website. 

Customer Portal

After payment for our services, a personal portal will be created for the customer. The customer shall receive a tailored ID and password to access the portal. All the main files exchanged with the customer will be uploaded on the personal portal of the customer. The personal portal page is a virtual space where the customer can access all the files produced by us together with some administration documents. On the personal portal the customer can also view the estimated deadline of the project and follow the progression of the work on the different phases.

Most importantly the final files shall be delivered to the customer at the personal portal. The customer shall have access to his or her personal portal now and in the future to enable him or her have constant access to the final files.
